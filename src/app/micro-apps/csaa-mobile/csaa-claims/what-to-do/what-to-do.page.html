Depending on your state, it may be required by law to notify law enforcement and submit a report after a crash.

Record accident details including the date, time of day and location.


Also note these items:
  • Other driver’s contact information
  • Vehicle make and model
  • Insurance company and policy number
  • Get driver’s license numbers if proof of insurance isn’t provided.

If possible, take photos for all vehicles involved of:

  • Vehicle damage
  • License plate
  • Location of accident

Give only your name and insurance provider and policy number, do not give out your address or allow your driver’s license to be photographed.

Before you have a claim, creating a home inventory of your most valuable possessions (including date, purchase price and location of purchase) may also help with the claims process in the future. Keep the list or video in a safe or a separate location.

Make a list of what was damaged or stolen. If property or items are damaged, take photos or videos.
